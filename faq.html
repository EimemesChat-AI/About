Frequently Asked Questions

What is EimemesChat AI?
EimemesChat AI is an AI‑powered conversational agent designed to preserve and promote the Kuki language and its dialects. It can translate, answer questions, and engage in culturally aware conversations.
Is the AI free to use?
Yes, our beta version is completely free. As we grow, we may introduce premium features, but core language tools will remain accessible to everyone.
Which dialects do you support?
We currently support Kuki (Thadou) and are actively working on Paite, Gangte, Vaiphei, and other Zo dialects. Our goal is to include as many as possible.
How accurate is the translation?
Our models are trained on culturally rich datasets, but like all AI, they can make mistakes. We continuously improve with community feedback.
Can I contribute to the project?
Absolutely! We welcome native speakers, linguists, and developers. Please contact us at contribute@eimemeschat.ai to get involved.
How do you protect my data?
We follow strict privacy practices. Your conversations are encrypted and never sold. See our Privacy Policy for details.
