About EimemesChat AI

EimemesChat AI was founded with a single mission: to ensure that the Kuki language and its dialects thrive in the age of artificial intelligence. We believe that language is the soul of a culture, and its disappearance from digital spaces means losing a part of humanity itself.

Our team consists of linguists, AI researchers, and community leaders who work together to build datasets that capture not just words, but the context, emotion, and cultural nuances behind them. We partner with native speakers across the Kuki-Zo communities to record, validate, and preserve authentic language use.

We are committed to open, ethical AI. All our models are designed to be transparent and accountable. We aim to release tools that are accessible to everyone – from students to elders – so that the language can be used in everyday digital life.

Join us in preserving our heritage for the generations to come.
